Lemma dsdp_matrix_rank1 u1 u2 u3 : (u1 != 0) || (u2 != 0) || (u3 != 0) -> \rank (dsdp_matrix u1 u2 u3) = 1.

Description

The DSDP coefficient matrix has rank 1 when at least one coefficient is nonzero. This establishes the linear system has exactly 1 constraint, giving 2 degrees of freedom.
